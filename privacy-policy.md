# Privacy Policy – ActionPlanner

Last updated: January 2026

ActionPlanner does not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal data.

## Data Collection
The app does not require users to create accounts and does not collect:
- Names
- Email addresses
- Phone numbers
- Location data
- Identifiers
- Usage analytics
- Cookies

## Data Usage
All functionality operates locally on the user’s device. No data is sent to external servers.

## Third-Party Services
ActionPlanner does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Children’s Privacy
The app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Changes
If this policy changes, updates will be posted on this page.
